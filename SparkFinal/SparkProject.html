What is Anime? In its most basic term anime is animation, which is not short for animation, but simply japanese animation. Oddly enough the term Anime in Japan, co-exist with just being a regular cartoon. Anime in its boradest term is any animated show or movie that uses signature aspects of Japanese-style animation, like vibrant colors, drammatic planning, and characteristic facial expressions.

In the United States most anime is classified as a niche for of entertainment, especially for adults.In Japan however it is culturally accepted and a fairly basic form of entertainment for most adults.

Subbed vs. Dubbed

Anime comes in two formats, subbed and dubbed. Subbed Anime plays the original Japanese ocal track for the show of the movie you're with Enlgish (or your native language) as subtitles.

Dubbed Anime on the other hand, features an english speaking cast of voice-over actors working with a translated script.

Anime VS. Manga

Manga are japanese comic books, which serve as the inspiration for most anime series. While in America manga only refers to comincs, in Japan , "manga" is just the japanse word for comic book. If your already a fan of anime and comins, or like to read. Consider reading manga and watching anime.
